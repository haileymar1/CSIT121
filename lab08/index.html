Bandeja Paisa!

Hailey Martinez

Ingredients are listed below

  • 1 cup of White Rice
  • 1 Can of Red kidney Beans
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Plantain
  • 8oz Sirlion
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Chimchurri steak sauce
  • Minced Garlic
  • 1 Sausage

Now to the instructions

  1. Dice up onion, put to simmer with oil in pot, add garlic as well
  2. Wash 1 cup of white rice and put into pot, then add 2 cups of water and salt
  3. Let rice cook for about 25 min on low heat
  4. Now clean the can of beans and add to pot with 2 cups of water, cut up tomatoes and add salt, then let cook for 25 min
  5. Starting to season the sirlion by adding chimichurri sauce in a bowl with the sirlion and add garlic and salt and a little bit of oil, let rest for 10 min
  6. Peel the plaintain and cut into slices then add to pan with oil, fry until darkened
  7. Place sausage in air fryer for about 15 min
  8. With the same oil from the plantain crack and fry 1 egg
  9. At this point you can add oil to the pan and cook the sirlion to your liking
  10. After sirlion gets off the grill, everything else should be finished and can be served

This is my favorite recipe because i am from colombia and it is very commonly eaten in my house, it has a large variety of protien in one plate. Also i am a very big fan of red meat lol

Prep time:15 min, Cook time:40-50 min
